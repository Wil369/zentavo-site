Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Zentavo is built with your privacy as a priority. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection & Storage

Zentavo does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or financial data on external servers. All data you enter into the app — including transactions, budgets, debts, income sources, and savings goals — is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data.

No Account Required

Zentavo does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or link any bank accounts or financial institutions to use the app.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. No analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers receive information from Zentavo.

No Tracking

Zentavo does not use cookies, tracking pixels, device fingerprinting, or any other tracking technologies.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored on your device, you can delete it at any time through the app's Settings using the Reset All Data option. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.

Children's Privacy

Zentavo does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@marketsoleil.com.
